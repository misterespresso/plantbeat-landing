PlantBeat Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2025

This privacy policy is applicable to the PlantBeat app (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, which was developed by Caffeinated Software LLC (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") as a Freemium service. This service is provided "AS IS".

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

The Application acquires the information you supply when you download and register the Application. Registration with the Service Provider is not mandatory. However, bear in mind that you might not be able to utilize some of the features offered by the Application unless you register with them.

The Service Provider may also use the information you provided them to contact you from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices and marketing promotions.

Plant Care and Community Data

When you use PlantBeat, we collect and store:

  • Plant Care Information: Your plant collection, care schedules, watering logs, and care history
  • Community Content: Posts, comments, images, and interactions you share in our plant identification and diagnosis communities
  • User Preferences: Plant care settings, notification preferences, and app customization choices
  • Care Photos: Images you upload for plant identification, diagnosis, or to save in your plant collection

AI and Camera Services

The Application may access your device's camera to capture photos of plants for identification and diagnosis purposes. Photos are processed using third-party AI services (Google Gemini) to provide plant identification and care recommendations. Images are not permanently stored unless you choose to save them to your plant collection.

Premium Subscription Data

If you subscribe to premium features, we process subscription status and payment information through third-party payment processors. We do not store your complete payment information directly.

Automatically Collected Information

In addition, the Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile device's unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, and information about the way you use the Application.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application collects your device's location, which helps the Service Provider determine your approximate geographical location and make use of in below ways:

  • Weather Integration: Location data is used to provide current weather conditions and forecasts to help you make informed plant care decisions
  • Regional Plant Care: Location information helps provide region-appropriate plant care recommendations based on your local climate and growing conditions
  • Geolocation Services: The Service Provider utilizes location data to provide features such as personalized content, relevant recommendations, and location-based services
  • Analytics and Improvements: Aggregated and anonymized location data helps the Service Provider analyze user behavior, identify trends, and improve the overall performance and functionality of the Application
  • Third-Party Services: Periodically, the Service Provider may transmit anonymized location data to external services. These services assist them in enhancing the Application and optimizing their offerings

Contact Information

For any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at administrator@plantbeat.com
